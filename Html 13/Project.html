The Resident Evil 2 remake is widely praised as a fantastic example of how to do a modern video game remake, successfully modernizing the classic survival horror experience while retaining its core elements. It balances thrilling action, challenging puzzles, and a gripping atmosphere to deliver a genuinely engaging and terrifying experience. While some minor issues exist, such as occasional awkward enemy AI and a slightly tedious element in unlocking alternate scenarios, the overall consensus is that the game is a masterpiece of the genre.
